Normal Skin Type

Normal skin is essentially skin that is well balanced; it is not too dry or oily, not too sensitive and has very few imperfections. Characterized by small pores, an even skin tone and soft texture, normal skin is what many would consider to be the ideal skin type.

Skin concerns:
  • Dryness – Lack of water in the skin can make it feel tight or dull.
  • Mild Sensitivity – Occasional redness or irritation due to environmental changes or harsh products.
  • Fine Lines – Early signs of aging, especially around the eyes and mouth.
  • Uneven Skin Tone – Minor pigmentation or dullness due to sun exposure or lifestyle factors.
  • Breakouts – Occasional pimples, usually due to stress, diet, or hormonal changes.
