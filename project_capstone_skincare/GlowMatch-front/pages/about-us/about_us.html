About Us

GlowMatch – Your Personalized Skincare Guide At GlowMatch, we understand that finding the right skincare products can be overwhelming. With countless options available, many young consumers struggle with trial and error, leading to wasted money, time, and frustration. That’s why we created a smarter way to shop for skincare, one that’s built around your unique skin type.

GlowMatch

The Check Ingredients feature analyzes skincare product ingredients and calculates their compatibility with the user’s skin type. By uploading an image or entering ingredients manually, users receive a percentage match score that indicates how well the product suits their skin and the benefits of those ingredients.
